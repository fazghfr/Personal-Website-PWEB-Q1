Nasi Gonjleng

authentic food from cilegon

Nasi Gonjleng, an authentic dish from Cilegon, Indonesia, features fragrant rice served with a meat dish, emping crackers, and pickles. The rice is cooked with a blend of spices and coconut milk, giving it a creamy sweetness.The form and taste are similar to samin rice or kebuli rice typical of the Middle East. This menu is believed to be the result of a fusion of two cultures. It's traditionally wrapped in banana leaves for added flavor and warmth. The accompanying meat dish, often chicken or beef, is prepared with a flavorful sauce. Emping crackers, made from melinjo nuts, provide a satisfying crunch, and pickles add a tangy contrast to the dish. Together, these elements create a delightful and well-balanced culinary experience unique to Cilegon.
