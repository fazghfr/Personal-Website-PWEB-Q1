Cilegon is not just an industrial city; it is a place that holds a special surprise for nature enthusiasts and beach lovers. Nestled along its coastline, you'll discover one of West Java's most renowned beaches: Anyer Beach. With its golden sands, tranquil waters, and captivating shoreline views, Anyer Beach offers an idyllic escape from the city's hustle and bustle. Whether you're seeking relaxation, engaging in water sports, or simply witnessing a breathtaking sunset, Anyer Beach has it all.

About this Place


Anyer Beach is a popular coastal town in Banten, Indonesia, located about 38 km from Serang City1. It's known for its clean, white sand and calm waves1. The beach faces west, offering a view of Mt. Rakata, the remnant of Mt. Krakatau that exploded in 1831. Anyer Beach is a popular resort for Jakartans and offers a variety of water sports such as jet skiing, speed boating, and parasailing.

On tripadvisor, the rating for this beach is 3.5. Which is pretty good if you are looking to relax and run away from the city's hustle and bustle.
